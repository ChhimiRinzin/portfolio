Online food ordering

Project Overview

The Product

Online food ordering is a food ordering platform where you can order food instantly from the convenience of your phone with the goal of making its customers happy with satisfying meals, snacks, and beverages.p>

Project Duration

February to May 2024

The Problem

The objective was to create a website that would enable consumers to quickly and simply order meals from restaurant at their convenience.

The Goal

Figure out if online food ordering can really solves customerâ€™s time when ordering snacks , drinks and food online.

My Role

User Experience Designer responsible for food ordering platform.

Responsibilities

Conducting interviews, wireframing, prototyping, usability studies, and iterating on designs.

Pain Points

Pain Point 1

Existing food ordering platforms do not have enough accessibility considerations. E.g. compatibility with assistive technologies.

Pain Point 2

By relying heavily on texts, some apps fail to consider that some of their users cannot speak/read English.

Pain Point 3

Food ordering platforms are not very easy to use. So people with limited digital literacy struggle complete the order process.p>

Pain Point 4

Busy professional do not have the time to prepare meals at home especially on weekdays when they are working.p>
