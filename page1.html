Mobile Application

A Camping supply store

Project Overview

The Product

A camping supply app developed to enhance the outdoor adventure experience for camping enthusiasts.

Project Duration

February to May 2024

The Problem

Camping enthusiasts often find challenges when it comes to finding high-quality camping gear that suits their specific needs.

The Goal

To create a user-friendly mobile application that consolidates all essential camping resources into one platform.

My Role

Creating a seamless and engaging user experience that enhances the outdoor adventure for camping enthusiasts.

Responsibilities

Conducting interviews, wireframing, prototyping, usability studies, and iterating on designs.

Pain Points

Pain Point 1

Need to buy items that will make my camping experience better.

Pain Point 2

How will I ensure that the quality of the items is good?

Pain Point 3

Want all the related items at one place since they don't have enough time to go to different places.

Pain Point 4

How will I know what items I will need?
